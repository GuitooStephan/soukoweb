Frequently Asked Questions

  • Store

    How to create a store
    • 1
      Click on the button labelled "Create store" located in your top navigation bar
    • 2
      Fill the "Create account" form by filling the required fields. Click on the button "Create account".
    • 3
      Fill the "Create your store" form by filling the required fields. Click on the button "Create store".
    • 4
      A link will be sent to your email.
    • 5
      Kindly click on the link sent to your email to activate your account. You will be redirected to the login page where you can login with your credentials.

    Where can you get the insights
    • 1
      Login your account
    • 2
      The insights should be on your dashboard
  • Products

    How to create a product
    • 1
      Login your account
    • 2
      Click on the button labelled "Add Product" located in your top navigation bar.
    • 3
      Fill the "Add product" form by filling the required fields. Click on the button "Add product".
    • 4
      The product should appear in your products ( Accessible by clicking on the link "Products" in your side navigation bar ).

    How to remove a product
    • 1
      Login your account
    • 2
      Click on the link "Products" located in your side navigation bar to view your products.
    • 3
      Click on the vertical three points to view the actions. And click on the option "Delete" to delete the product.
  • Customers

    How to add a customer
    • 1
      Login your account
    • 2
      Click on the link "Customers" located in your side navigation bar to view your customers.
    • 3
      Click on the button labelled "Add Customer" located next the title "Customers".
    • 4
      Fill the "Create customer" form by filling the required fields. Click on the button "Create customer".
    • 5
      The customer should appear in your customers list.
  • Orders

    How to record an order
    • 1
      Login your account
    • 2
      Click on the button "Record order" located in your top navigation bar.
    • 3
      Kindly ensure that the customer placing the order is registered in the platform. If not you could click on the link "Quickly add a new customer" to register the customer and continue the process.
    • 4
      Fill the remaining required fields of the form. Click on the button "Record order".
    • 5
      The order should appear in your orders ( Accessible by clicking on the link "Orders" in your side navigation bar ).
  • E-shop

    How to get your personalized e-commerce link ?
    • 1
      Login your account
    • 2
      Click on the vertical three dots (...) button located in your top navigation bar.
    • 3
      Click on the option "Copy E-Commerce Link" to copy the link on your clipboard.
    • 4
      Now you can paste the link anywhere you would like ( by right-clicking on your mouse and selecting the option "Paste" )
    • Anyone with this link should be able to access your e-commerce page.

    How does the personalized e-commerce work ?
    • The personalized E-commerce page allows you to easily sell your products to your customers.
      Let's say you have a customer that is interested by your products and would like to purchase them.
    • 1
      Copy the E-commerce link to your clipboard. ( Check the question above for more details ).
    • 2
      Forward the link to the customer.
    • 3
      Once he clicks on the link, he will access your e-commerce page. He will be able to select the products he wants and place his order.
    • 4
      He will be asked for some personal information before placing the order. If it is his/her first time placing an order, the platform will required a confirmation of the email address before validating the order.
      ( A link will be sent to his email. He will have to click on it for his order to be confirmed ).
    • 5
      When the order is confirmed, the admin of the store should receive an email with the order id.
      He/She can login, check the order and contact the customer for delivery
    • Unfortunately, we don't yet accept payments on the platform. We are currently working on it.
